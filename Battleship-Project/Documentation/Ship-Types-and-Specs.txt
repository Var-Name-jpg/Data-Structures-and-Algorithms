Each ship type has it's own specific parameters.

Available Ship Types:
- Carrier
- Battleship
- Destroyer
- Submarine
- Patrol Boat

Ship Sizes:
- Carrier: 5 Tiles
- Battleship: 4 Tiles
- Destroyer: 3 Tiles
- Submarine: 3 Tiles
- Patrol Boat: 2 Tiles

Ships can be placed either vertically or horizontally.

Ships cannot go out of bounnds or intersect with other ships.

The playing field is 10x10 tiles:
- Starts at (0, 0)
- Ends at (9, 9)
